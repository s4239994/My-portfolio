The gap

Trends move in weeks. Design tools move in quarters.

By the time a template category catches up to a trend, three more have already replaced it. Small creators and brand accounts end up either off-trend or manually rebuilding a palette from a screenshot every single time. Magic Trends closes that gap automatically, inside the tool they're already using.

In three steps

From spotted to posted.

01

A trend is identified

Our design team curates and verifies each aesthetic drop — no scraping social platforms, just documented, real trend cycles.

02

Magic Trends builds the kit

A palette, font pairing, and layout pack are generated and matched against your existing brand kit.

03

You remix and post

Open it straight in the Canva editor, already laid out, already on-trend, already yours to edit.
