Why this exists

This is a concept feature, designed and built as a self-directed portfolio project — not an official Canva product. It exists to answer one question: what's a feature Canva could plausibly ship next, built the way their own Magic Studio family is built (Magic Design, Dream Lab, Magic Write, Magic Charts), and designed with the same craft their newsroom launch pages get?

Every trend, palette, and step on this site is real and specific — nothing here is a lorem-ipsum mockup. What isn't real is the backend: no emails are actually collected, and this isn't affiliated with or endorsed by Canva.
