For creators

You don't have time to be a trend forecaster and a graphic designer.

You know when something's trending the second it hits your feed — the problem is turning that instinct into a finished post before the trend moves on. Magic Trends skips the "screenshot it and guess the colors" step entirely.

  • Jump on a trend the same day it starts moving, not two weeks later
  • Get a palette and layout that's actually accurate, not eyeballed
  • Still fully editable — it's a starting point, not a straitjacket

For brand accounts

You need to look current without looking like you're chasing.

A brand jumping on every trend looks desperate; a brand that never does looks stale. Magic Trends merges your existing brand kit with the trend palette, so the output reads as "on-trend within reason," not a full identity switch for one post.

  • Trend-aware content that still passes brand review
  • One click to preview a trend against your locked brand kit
  • A documented, human-curated source list — nothing scraped, nothing risky to cite
