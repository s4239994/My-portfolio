A genre hub for original mini-games -- like a streaming service, except
each genre is something you play instead of watch, and each one takes a
familiar genre and inverts one core assumption instead of cloning an
existing game.

**Status:** Action ("Overload") is fully working. Comedy, Finance, Horror,
and Thriller are in progress, being added one at a time.

## The concept

- **Action -- Overload** -- your energy bar isn't health, it's fuel.
  Getting hit by an enemy refuels you. Getting hit while you're already
  almost full overloads you -- game over. Pulse attacks clear enemies
  safely but cost the same energy you need to survive.
- **Comedy, Finance, Horror, Thriller** -- each planned around one inverted
  mechanic for that genre (see the [project README](https://github.com/s4239994/My-portfolio/tree/main/mixtape) for details as they land).

## Stack

Java, Swing -- no external dependencies, no build tool required beyond the
JDK itself.
